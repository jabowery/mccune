mccune Examples: Ternary Relations in Lattices

These theorems are based on the following paper.
  • R. Padmanabhan, "On Some Ternary Relations in Lattices", Colloquium Mathematicum 15:195-198, 1966.
This work is part of an ongoing project of Padmanabhan, Veroff, and McCune.
